4. Detailed Information Collected

Location

We use device location to provide the core functionality of UnveilHex — namely, revealing map zones and tracking exploration progress. Location is used locally on your device to calculate which zones you have visited.

When is location collected? Only when you grant the app permission to access location. You can disable location permissions in your device settings; doing so will limit the app's functionality.

Device & Usage Data

We may use ad networks and analytics SDKs that collect non‑identifying technical information such as:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version and crash reports
  • Basic usage metrics (e.g., session length, in‑app events)

This data is used to improve the app, measure performance, and (if applicable) personalize ads.

Advertisements

UnveilHex shows in‑app advertisements. Ad networks may collect information about your device and activity to serve ads and measure advertising performance. We do not control the data collection practices of third‑party ad networks — see Third‑party services below.
